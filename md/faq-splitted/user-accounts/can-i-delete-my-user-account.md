### Can I delete my user account?

Yes, you can delete your Account

- go to [Your Account Settings](https://www.wikidot.com/account/settings) and go to the **Account Settings**
- click **Delete Account** at the bottom of the list and follow the instructions. You will receive a confirmation e-mail and after clicking on the link in the e-mail, you will be prompted to provide your password. After doing so, your account will be deleted.

**Note that deleting an account is not equal to deleting Wikis.** If you will delete an account, you Wiki will still be hosted by Wikidot.com.
