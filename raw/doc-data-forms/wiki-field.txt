
<div class="page-title">
                    The 'wiki' field type
                </div>
<div id="page-content">
<p>Works like text but lets the user enter wiki syntax.</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[form]]
fields:
  version:
    label: Fancy text field
    type: wiki
[[/form]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>The specific properties you can use on a wiki field:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>width</strong>: specifies the width of the field in the dataform.</li>
<li><strong>height</strong>: specifies the height of the field in the dataform.</li>
</ul>
