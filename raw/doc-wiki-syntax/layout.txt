<h2 id="toc0"><span><a name="tab-view"></a>Tab view</span></h2>
<p>Tab view is a container that creates some clickable tabs that allow to switch between content to show.</p>
<p><strong>NOTE: TabView breaks TOCs, anchor links and back button</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>you can't link to anchor inside of a tab</li>
<li>TOC won't link properly to any header inside of a tab</li>
<li>if you click a link from within a tab and go back, you will be always shown the first tab</li>
</ul>
<p>To generate a <em>tabview</em>, i.e. a set of tabs, the following syntax can be used:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[tabview]]
[[tab Title of Tab No. 1]]
Content of Tab No. 1.
[[/tab]]
[[tab Title of Tab No. 2]]
Content of Tab No. 2.
[[/tab]]
[[tab Title of Tab No. 3]]
Content of Tab No. 3.
[[/tab]]
[[/tabview]]</code>
</pre></div>
