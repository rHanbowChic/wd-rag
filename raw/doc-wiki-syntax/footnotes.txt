<div id="page-content">
<p>To make footnotes in the text use <tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">[[footnote]]</span></tt> block. To force the list of footnotes<br/>
to appear <span style="text-decoration: underline;">not</span> at the end of the page, use <tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">[[footnoteblock]]</span></tt>.</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>Some text[[footnote]]And a small footnote.[[/footnote]]. Here we go
with another footnote[[footnote]]Content of another footnote.[[/footnote]].

[[footnoteblock]]</code>
</pre></div>
