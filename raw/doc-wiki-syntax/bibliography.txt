<p>The bibliography block is defined by <tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">[[bibliography]]...[[/bibliography]]</span></tt>. Each bibliography item has the form:<br/>
<tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">label : full reference</span></tt><br/>
To cite a bibliography entry use <tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">((bibcite</span> <em>label</em>))</tt>.</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>The first pulsar was observed by J. Bell and A. Hewish [((bibcite bell))]. Another reference [see ((bibcite guy))].

[[bibliography]]
: bell : Bell, J.; Hewish, A.; Pilkington, J. D. H.; Scott, P. F.; and Collins, R. A. //Observation of a Rapidly Pulsating Radio Source.// Nature 217, 709, 1968.
: guy : Guy, R. K. //Modular Difference Sets and Error Correcting Codes.// §C10 in Unsolved Problems in Number Theory, 2nd ed. New York: Springer-Verlag, pp. 118-121, 1994.
[[/bibliography]]</code>
</pre></div>
