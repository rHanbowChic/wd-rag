<p>This is equivalent to:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[social blinklist,blogmarks,connotea,del.icio.us,digg,fark,feedmelinks,furl,linkagogo,newsvine,netvouz,reddit,simpy,spurl,wists,yahoomyweb,facebook]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>You can also choose only selected services, e.g. to show digg, furl, del.icio.us and Facebook use:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[social digg,furl,del.icio.us,facebook]]</code>
</pre></div>
