<div id="page-content">
<p>To make a heading start a line with a "plus". Make as many pluses as the heading level you want to get.</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>+ Level 1 Heading
++ Level 2 Heading
+++ Level 3 Heading
++++ Level 4 Heading
+++++ Level 5 Heading
++++++ Level 6 Heading</code>
</pre></div>
