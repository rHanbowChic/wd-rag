                    Literal Text
                </div>
<div class="breadcrumb">
<a href="/doc:start">Documentation</a> » <a href="/doc-wiki-syntax:start">Wiki Syntax</a> » Literal Text
                    </div>
<div id="page-content">
<p>If you want to escape parsing and produce raw text enclose it in double @@ characters.</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>This //text// gets **parsed**.

@@This //text// does not get **parsed**.@@</code>
</pre></div>
<p>This <em>text</em> gets <strong>parsed</strong>.</p>
<p><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">This //text// does not get **parsed**.</span></p>
</div><div id="action-area" style="display: none;"></div>
