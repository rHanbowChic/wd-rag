<div id="page-content">
<p>If you do care about typography there are a few ways to improve it in your text:</p>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>you type</th>
<th>you get</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">``quotation'' </span></tt></td>
<td>“quotation”</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">`quotation' </span></tt></td>
<td>‘quotation’</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">,,quotation''</span></tt></td>
<td>„quotation”</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">&lt;&lt;quotation&gt;&gt;</span></tt></td>
<td>«quotation»</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">&gt;&gt;quotation&lt;&lt;</span></tt></td>
<td>»quotation«</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">dots...</span></tt></td>
<td>dots…</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt><span style="white-space: pre-wrap;">em -- dash</span></tt></td>
<td>em — dash</td>
</tr>
</table>
<p>Note: em dash works only when surrounded by spaces.</p>
