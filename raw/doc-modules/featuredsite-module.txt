<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>FeaturedSite module is very similar to SiteGrid, but it's displaying <strong>only one</strong> wiki site thumbnail and the thumbnail is much bigger.</p>
<p>This module has no attributes.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Example</span></h2>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module FeaturedSite]]
community.wikidot.com
[[/module]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>Which transfers to…</p>
