<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>This module will allow your users to look up how particular Members joined this Wiki. In particular it will display a chain of invitations.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<p>No attributes required.</p>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>Check how particular Members joined this Wiki.

[[module WhoInvited]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>And looks like this (this will not work very well within this Wiki because there are not many members here):</p>
<p>Check how particular Members joined this Wiki.</p>
