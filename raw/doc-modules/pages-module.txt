<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>The <em>Pages</em> module is able to list pages within a site or within a category.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>attribute</th>
<th>required</th>
<th>allowed values</th>
<th>default</th>
<th>description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>category</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>name of an existing category</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>limits the listing to the specified category</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>details</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"true"</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>print extra information about pages</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>preview</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"true"</td>
<td>none</td>
<td><span style="text-decoration: line-through;">prints a short preview of the page</span> (This parameter is temporarily ignored due to performance reasons. It will come back soon)</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>order</td>
<td>no</td>
<td><tt>dateCreatedDesc</tt><br/>
<tt>dateCreatedAsc</tt><br/>
<tt>dateEditedDesc</tt><br/>
<tt>dateEditedAsc</tt><br/>
<tt>titleDesc</tt><br/>
<tt>titleAsc</tt></td>
<td><tt>titleAsc</tt></td>
<td>selects ordering of the pages</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>limit</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>any positive integer</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>how many pages to return; if you omit this all pages will be listed</td>
</tr>
</table>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<p>Print only pages from the <em>_default</em> category with details:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Pages category="_default" details="true"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>Print all pages with details and preview:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Pages details="true" preview="true"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>10 most recently edited pages</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Pages order="dateEditedDesc" limit="10"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>10 most recently created pages</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Pages order="dateCreatedDesc" limit="10"]]</code>
</pre></div>
