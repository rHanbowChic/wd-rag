<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>The SearchUsers module lets you search all Wikidot users by login id, email address, or full name. You can place the SearchUsers module itself on any page, but your site <em>must</em> contain a page called "search:users" that (also) contains the SearchUsers module.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<p>The SearchUsers module does not allow any attributes.</p>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Example</span></h2>
<p>On your site start page:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>++ Search all Wikidot users
[[module SearchUsers]]
= (enter email address or nick or real name)</code>
</pre></div>
<p>On your site's search:users page:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module SearchUsers]]</code>
</pre></div>
