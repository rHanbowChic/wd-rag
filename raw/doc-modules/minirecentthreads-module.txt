<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Displays most recent forum threads in a forum suitable to be included e.g. within the welcome page. The list items contain thread title, date started and number of posts.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>attribute</th>
<th>required</th>
<th>allowed values</th>
<th>default</th>
<th>description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>limit</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>positive integer</td>
<td>5</td>
<td>how many items to display?</td>
</tr>
</table>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>++ Most recent forum threads

[[module MiniRecentThreads limit="3"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>simply displays 3 most recent forum threads.</p>
