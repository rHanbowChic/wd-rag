<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Allows registered users (these having a valid wikidot account) to apply for membership in a specific site. Applications can be reviewed by site administrators via <a href="/doc:managesite-module">ManageSite</a> module under a specific tab.</p>
<p>Site administrators must allow applying by checking an option in the site administration module.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<p>No attributes required</p>
