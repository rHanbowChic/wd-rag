<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Allows registered users (these having a valid wikidot account) to automatically become members of the site provided they know the password.</p>
<p>The password itself must be configured using the <a href="/doc:managesite-module">ManageSite</a> module and the password-based membership sign-in must be enabled.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<p>No attributes required.</p>
