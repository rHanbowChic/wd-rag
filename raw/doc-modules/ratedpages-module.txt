<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Displays top-rated pages. Also a category can be specified to limit the results.</p>
<div class="wiki-note">
<p>This module is under development and not yet complete.<br/>
But should work as described below.</p>
</div>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>attribute</th>
<th>required</th>
<th>allowed values</th>
<th>default</th>
<th>description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>category</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>valid category name</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>limits reported pages to a single category</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>order</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"date-created-asc", "date-created-desc", "rating-asc", "rating-desc"</td>
<td>"rating-desc"</td>
<td></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>minRating</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>integer</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>limits the results to the pages having rating equal or above this limit</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>maxRating</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>integer</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>limits the results to the pages having rating equal or below this limit</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>limit</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>positive integer</td>
<td>10</td>
<td>limits number of displayed pages</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>comments</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"true"/"yes"</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>display number of comments too</td>
</tr>
</table>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<p>Display top-rated pages from the category <tt>rateit</tt></p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module RatedPages category="rateit" limit="20" comments="true" minRating="0"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>Display "most hated" pages:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module RatedPages category="rateit" order="rate-asc" limit="20" comments="true" maxRating="-1"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>Display new submissions:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module RatedPages category="rateit" order="date-created-desc" limit="20" comments="true"]]</code>
</pre></div>
