<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>SiteGrid module gives you a possibility to create a grid of thumbnails of sites on Wikidot with a descriptions and some details in a form of pop-up when you move your mouse cursor over thumbnail.</p>
<p>SiteGrid module is sorting thumbnails randomly. It's used on the main Wikidot page to list featured sites.</p>
<p>Inside the module you have to put a list either of the names of sites, i.e. <strong>community</strong> (if the address is <em>community.wikidot.com</em>) or full address, i.e. <strong>community.wikidot.com</strong> or <strong>www.digistan.org</strong> (if a site has a custom domain).</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>Attribute</th>
<th>Required</th>
<th>Allowed values</th>
<th>Default</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>limit</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>any integer</td>
<td>none</td>
<td>Limiting the number of displayed thumbnails from the predefined list</td>
</tr>
</table>
