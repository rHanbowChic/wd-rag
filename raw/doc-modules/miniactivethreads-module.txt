<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Displays the most active threads within the last 7 days in a compact form. Thread title, date started and total number of posts is displayed.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>attribute</th>
<th>required</th>
<th>allowed values</th>
<th>default</th>
<th>description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><tt>limit</tt></td>
<td>no</td>
<td>positive integer</td>
<td>5</td>
<td>how many threads to print</td>
</tr>
</table>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<p>The code:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>+++ Most active topics (last week)

[[module MiniActiveThreads limit="5"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>displays the most active forum threads within last 7 days.</p>
