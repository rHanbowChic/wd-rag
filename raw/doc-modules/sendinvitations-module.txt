<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>Allows Members of a Wiki to send email invitations to their friends/coworkers to join the Wiki. This is a nice thing if you want your community to grow quickly.</p>
<p>For the module to work it must be enabled under Site Manager » Members » Let Users invite</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<p>No attributes required.</p>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>Invite your friends to this Wiki!

[[module SendInvitations]]</code>
