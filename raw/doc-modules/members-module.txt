<h2 id="toc0"><span>Description</span></h2>
<p>This module is used to list members of the site.</p>
<h2 id="toc1"><span>Attributes</span></h2>
<table class="wiki-content-table">
<tr>
<th>attribute</th>
<th>required</th>
<th>allowed values</th>
<th>default</th>
<th>description</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>group</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"members"<br/>
"admins"<br/>
"moderators"</td>
<td>"members"</td>
<td>limits the list to the specified group</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>showSince</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"no" or "false"</td>
<td>"yes" for group="members"</td>
<td>does not show the date joined; valid only for group="members"</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>order</td>
<td>no</td>
<td>"userId", "userIdDesc", "joined", "joinedDesc", "name", "nameDesc"</td>
<td>"joined"</td>
<td>sort Members by name (alphabetically), by user ID or date of joining</td>
</tr>
</table>
<h2 id="toc2"><span>Examples</span></h2>
<p>List all members of the site:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Members]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>List only site administrators:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Members group="admins"]]</code>
</pre></div>
<p>List only moderators:</p>
<div class="code">
<pre>
<code>[[module Members group="moderators"]]</code>
</pre></div>
